A Statistic on Pizza in United States Based On A Dataset By Datafiniti

Surveyed Pizza Shop in United States

Based on a dataset by Datafiniti, we can observe all the surveyed pizza shops along with the pizzas they offered as a mean to evaluate the variety of pizzas that is available in United States. The map visualisation above shows all the surveyed pizza shops in United States, counted by the state (province) they are located in. The exact location of pizza shops are shown in the map as well along with their restaurant name and address. From the map, we can observe that higher populated states will contribute to a higher density of pizza shops, such as New York, California, Texas, Pennsylvania etc.

Total Number of Pizza Shops in Each State

The bar chart above shows the number of pizza shops surveyed in each state, sorted from the highest population density to the lowest population density in the states. As observed from the visualisation, there's a clear declining trend on the number of pizza shops over the course of decreasing population density among the states, which is a fairly comprehensible and logical outcome.

Most Surveyed Pizza Shops in United States

Not necessarily the ranking for the most franchised pizza shops in United States due to the limited sample size provided by the dataset, but it shows some of the most popular pizza shops available from states to states in United States. Among the pizza shops, Papa John takes the easy lead in this chart, appearing for 70 times in this dataset.

The Average Price for Popular Pizza Types in Each City

BBQ Chicken, Buffalo, Cheese, Hawaiian, Margherita, Meat, Pepperoni, Veggie: these are all the familiar names that will call back to your sweetest recent memories you have with a pizza. However, they do come with different prices among different states. The visualisation above shows the average prices of each popular pizza type in different state, sorted by from highest population density to lowest, along with the average prices for each pizza types in general (United States). According to the charts, Meats Pizza has the highest average price among the popular pizza, while cheese pizza has the lowest average price among them.
